Yverdon-les-Bains

Located between the Jura mountains and Lake Neuchâtel, Yverdon-les-Bains is the most important spa resort in French-speaking Switzerland.
The climate of Yverdon-les-Bains is mild (the town lies at an altitude of 433m/ 1420ft above sea level). The town and its surrounding region are a real paradise for fans of nature and water sports.
Yverdon-les-Bains offers high speed train connections to major Swiss cities such as:
  • Lausanne
  • Neuchâtel
  • Geneva
  • Balel
  • Zurich
